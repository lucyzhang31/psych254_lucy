Stanford Problem Solving Study

Thank you for your interest in the Stanford problem-solving study. This study examines how your academic and social experiences influence problem-solving. First, you will answer several demographic questions to determine whether you are eligible to participate in the study. If you are eligible to participate in the study, you will automatically progress to the study. During the study, you will be asked to solve several word puzzles and to think about yourself and others who are close to you. If you are not eligible to participate in the study, you will be exited from the study.

Legal information: By answering the following questions, you are participating in a study being performed by researchers in the Stanford Department of Psychology. If you have questions about this research, please contact Lucy Zhang at stanfordsubjects@gmail.com. You must be at least 18 years old to participate. Your participation in this research is voluntary. You may decline to answer any or all of the following questions. You may decline further participation, at any time, without adverse consequences. Your anonymity is assured; the researchers who have requested your participation will not receive any personally identifying information about you.
